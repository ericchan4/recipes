Hainanese Chicken

Description

Recipe for Hainanese chicken from dommelier on TikTok.

Chicken - Trim off skin for rice - Salt the chicken - Scallions, ginger, chicken boullion, water, white pepper powder, MSG (need enough for soup and rice) - Bring to a boil, then cook for 20 minutes on simmer - When chicken is done, add to a ice bath - Strain broth Rice - Render out chicken skin - Add in shallots and ginger - Add washed rice - Move to rice cooker - Add chicken broth Sauces - Chili garlic + broth - Soy Sauce + sugar - Ginger, scallion, garlic + oil

Ingredients

Steps

  • Chicken
    1. Trim off skin for rice
    2. Salt the chicken
    3. Scallions, ginger, chicken boullion, water, white pepper powder, MSG (need enough for soup and rice)
    4. Bring to a boil, then cook for 20 minutes on simmer
    5. When chicken is done, add to a ice bath
    6. Strain broth
  • Rice
    1. Render out chicken skin
    2. Add in shallots and ginger
    3. Add washed rice
    4. Move to rice cooker
    5. Add chicken broth
  • Sauces
    1. Chili garlic + broth
    2. Soy Sauce + sugar
    3. Ginger, scallion, garlic + oil
